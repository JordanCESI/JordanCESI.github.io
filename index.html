Project 3: Big Data

As with the hospital sector as a whole, the Cloud Healthcare Unit (CHU) has realized the value - indeed the necessity - of a major digital transformation.
And your department is being asked to help set up its own data warehouse that will enable the group to exploit the huge amount of data generated by the care management and FTP systems.
The aim is to be able to respond to the variety of user needs and requirements for access and analysis. For this, the UHC group expects :

  • A complete solution in terms of models, tools and architecture that allows data to be extracted and stored, and then explored and visualized according to different criteria
  • A solution for integrating data from distributed files into a single persistent source
  • Identify the needs of users (practitioners, head of establishment) in terms of data analysis for direct exploitation on the follow-up of patients at national level and in the long term
  • Recommendations in terms of integration tools, storage and adapted visualization software as well as data mining in complete security in order to promote better decision-making.
